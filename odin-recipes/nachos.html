Nachos

Description

Nachos are a popular Tex-Mex dish consisting of tortilla chips topped with melted cheese, and often additional savory toppings like refried beans, ground beef, or jalape√±os. They are commonly served as an appetizer or snack, and are known for their crunchy texture and customizable toppings.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Beans
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Pickled Jalapenos
  • Fresh Toppings (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cook your ground beef in a skillet or pan over medium heat for 5 minutes.
  2. Add taco seasoning to the ground beef and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  3. Cook your refried beans with the ground beef and stir until combined.
  4. Spread a layer of tortilla chips on a rimmed baking tray and add a layer of your ground beef mixture.
  5. Add cheese, jalapenos, and any additional fresh toppings you have.
  6. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes until the cheese is melted.
  7. Add dollops of sour cream on top and serve.
